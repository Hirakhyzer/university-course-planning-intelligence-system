# Advising and governance policy

This project supports academic advising research only.

It must not be used to automatically:

- enroll or remove a student from a course
- deny access to a required course
- certify degree completion
- change financial-aid eligibility
- rank students for scarce course seats
- replace advisor, registrar, department-chair, accessibility, or student input

Required human checks for real use:

- advisor review of recommendations
- student confirmation of goals and constraints
- registrar validation of prerequisites and degree rules
- accessibility and accommodation review
- fairness and appeal procedures
- audit retention and deletion policy
